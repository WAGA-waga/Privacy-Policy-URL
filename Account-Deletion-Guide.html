Account Deletion Guide

Thank you for choosing our application. The game use Unity Game Services for account management. Below are the steps to delete your game account.:

Steps

  • Please use the email address you used to create your account.
  • Send an email to: wagawagastudio@gmail.com
  • In the subject line of your email, write [Request to Delete Account].
  • In the body of your email, provide your game username or any identification information related to your account.
  • We will process your request and respond to you within a reasonable time frame.

Data Handling

According to our privacy policy, when you request to delete your account, we will:

  • Delete all personal information associated with your account.
  • Certain data may be retained in compliance with legal regulations (e.g., for tax reporting purposes), but this data will no longer be associated with your personal information.
